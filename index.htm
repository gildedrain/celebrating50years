Who could have imagined fifty years ago that empty farmland atop Onondaga Hill would become a college campus, one of the largest in the SUNY system, serving over 12,000 students? On its Fiftieth Anniversary today's Onondaga Community College is an unmistakable CNY landmark that has been steadfast in its dedication to students and their success.
